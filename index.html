Interesting facts about a camel

  • Camel is a domestic animal in desert.
  • Its average life is 45-60 years.
  • The suitable weather for camel is hot and dry.

Interesting facts about a chameleon

  • Chameleons are a family of lizards.
  • They have parrot-like zygodactylous feet.
  • many of these can change colour.

Interesting facts about a pigeon

  • Pigeons are intelligent birds.
  • They can identify the same person on 2 different images.
  • Pigeons can easily detect distant storms, earthquakes and volcanic eruptions.

Interesting facts about a teddy-bear

  • Teddy Bear is a stuffed toy in the form of Bear.
  • Teddy Day is a day for sharing gifts between the two closed people.
  • Offering Teddy Bears to the beloved people is the main tradition of the day.

Interesting facts about a Panda

  • They have great camouflage for their environment.
  • They spend a lot of their day eating.
  • Bamboo is critical to their diet.

Interesting facts about a Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • Most have red-orange eyes.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
